Our story

Why We Exist.

Most owners of successful service businesses have spent a decade or more building something real. When it's time to sell, they deserve an advisor who understands that — not just the numbers, but the team, the culture, and what comes after.

Summit Mergers was built for that moment. We represent owners through the most significant financial decision of their lives — with the discretion of a trusted advisor, the discipline of a structured process, and the market knowledge that comes from working both sides of the table.

We work exclusively on behalf of sellers. That's not a detail — it's the foundation of everything we do.

How we operate

Three Things We Never Compromise On.

01

Discretion

Your employees, customers, and competitors should never know a transaction is underway until you're ready to tell them. We treat confidentiality not as a service feature but as a professional obligation.

02

Alignment

We operate on a success-fee basis, paid by the acquirer upon closing. You pay nothing, ever. Our incentive is not to close any deal — it's to close the right deal at the best possible price.

03

Preparation

The owners who achieve the best outcomes almost always started the conversation early. We help you understand your business's true market value and build toward a premium exit — whether that's six months or three years away.

Our focus

We Represent One Side of Every Deal.

We work exclusively with sellers — owners of profitable, established service businesses with annual revenues between $2M and $50M. We do not represent buyers. That focus is what allows us to be genuinely, unconditionally on your side.
