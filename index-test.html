Common
Questions

We believe in complete transparency. If you have a question we haven't answered, reach out directly.

What types of businesses do you work with?
+
We specialize in profitable service-based businesses with annual revenues between $2M–$50M. This includes HVAC, access control and security, fencing, professional services, technology, healthcare services, and other established enterprises with strong fundamentals.
How long does the typical sale process take?
+
Most transactions close within 6–12 months from initial engagement. The timeline varies by business complexity and market conditions, but we work efficiently while ensuring you achieve the best possible outcome.
How do you maintain confidentiality?
+
Discretion is paramount. We use NDAs with all potential buyers, present your business anonymously in initial outreach, and carefully manage all information flow. Your employees, clients, and competitors won't know unless you choose to tell them.
Do I pay any fees as the seller?
+
No. We operate on a success-fee basis paid exclusively by the acquirer upon closing. You pay nothing — ever. This ensures our interests are perfectly aligned with yours.
What if I'm not ready to sell for a few years?
+
Perfect. Many of our best relationships start years before a transaction. We can help you understand your current valuation, identify areas to enhance it, and position you for success when the timing is right.
How do you find qualified buyers?
+
We maintain an active network of institutional buyers — private equity firms, family offices, and strategic acquirers. We also conduct targeted research to identify buyers specifically seeking businesses with your profile.
